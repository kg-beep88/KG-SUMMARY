KG ADDRESS SHIFT CALENDAR V5.1 — VERY SIMPLE
================================================

WHAT CHANGED
------------
1. The calendar entry shows the ADDRESS as the first line.
2. No Work Name.
3. No start/end time.
4. No hours and no hourly rate.
5. Worker, Foreman and Subcon each count as 1 shift per day.
6. Delivery Order has no “From location” choice.
7. Summary shows Material cost, Manpower cost and Total cost separately.

UPLOAD
------
1. Extract the SAFE-UPGRADE ZIP.
2. Open your existing GitHub repository.
3. Add file > Upload files.
4. Upload all extracted files and replace the old ones.
5. Do NOT delete your existing supabase-config.js.
6. Commit changes.
7. Open the website and press Ctrl + F5.

USE
---
Calendar > click a date > Site entry.
Choose the address, dates and status.
Add Worker, Foreman, Subcon or All.
Type the pay for one shift.
Save.

For a delivery, press Delivery and choose only the delivery address.
